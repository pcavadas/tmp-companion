# Interoperability statement

TMP Companion is an **independently created** program that interoperates with a
Fender Tone Master Pro that **you own**, acting only on **your own device and
your own presets**. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by
Fender.

## What it does, and on what

Every operation targets the user's own hardware over USB and the user's own
preset data:

- read the connected unit's preset list, signal chain, songs, and setlists;
- level a preset's loudness, copy blocks between presets, and back up / restore
  presets — all on the user's own device.

It performs no preset distribution or sharing. There is no feature that
publishes, uploads, or redistributes presets or any Fender data.

## Legal basis (EU / France)

This project is developed and distributed under EU/French law, the governing
regime for the analysis below.

- **Interoperability right.** CPI L122-6-1 IV (transposing Software Directive
  2009/24/EC Art. 6) permits the acts needed to achieve interoperability of an
  independently created program with other programs/devices. CPI L331-5 bakes
  the interoperability carve-out into technical-protection-measure protection
  itself ("les mesures techniques ne peuvent s'opposer à l'interopérabilité").
  The "exchange and mutual use of information" in Directive 2009/24 recital 10
  includes writing back to the user's own device.
- **Technical protection measures.** To the extent any technical measure
  restricts a preset's functional data, the interoperability carve-out above
  (CPI L331-5) applies: technical measures cannot stand in the way of
  interoperability. The payload at stake is functional DSP-parameter data, not
  protected expressive content.
- **Trademarks.** Third-party names appear only nominatively, to describe
  compatibility and lineage (EUTMR 2017/1001 Art. 14 / CPI L713-6). See NOTICE.

## Scope and intent

This project exists for owner-side interoperability. Use it only with a Tone
Master Pro you own, on presets you own. Do not use it to circumvent access
controls on content you do not own, and do not extend it into preset
distribution or sharing.
